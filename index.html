Our Coffee Story

Founded in 2015, Brew Haven began as a passion project between two coffee enthusiasts. We source beans directly from sustainable farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Brazil.

Every cup is carefully crafted by our trained baristas who complete our intensive 4-week brewing program. We believe coffee should be an experience, not just a caffeine fix.

Visit us and taste the difference that small-batch roasting and genuine care makes in every sip.
